Integrated vINKo pen

The native version of the platform. A machine designed from the ground up around the integrated supply system, delivering a consistent experience and ready for professional-level scale.

External vINKo module

A compact module that attaches to compatible pen-type machines. It connects to the wireless power source through a magnetic connector and lets you turn the system on or off as you wish, without abandoning your current machine.

How your studio can adopt vINKo

vINKo is designed for studios that want to move towards an integrated machine without losing their investment in current machines. Thatâ€™s why the platform has two clear adoption paths.

1. vINKo pen from day one

The most direct way to enter the platform. You acquire the integrated vINKo pen and work with the native consumable ecosystem from day one.

2. External module for your pen

If your pen-type machine is compatible with the system connector, you can add vINKo as an external module that attaches to the body of the machine and is powered through a magnetic connector.

3. Gradual evolution

A studio can start by using the module on existing machines and, when timing makes sense, move to a fleet of integrated vINKo machines, keeping the same ecosystem and consumables.
