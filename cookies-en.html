Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website may store on your device to remember information (e.g., preferences or session). Similar technologies may also be used (e.g., localStorage, pixels, tags).

2. Does this website use cookies?

At this time, vinkosystem.com is built to operate with as few cookies as possible. Some features may involve third-party infrastructure (e.g., hosting/security), but our goal is to avoid marketing cookies.

3. Third-party cookies

If we later add tools that use cookies (e.g., advanced tracking, ads, or embedded integrations), we will update this policy and, where required, request consent via a banner or preferences panel.

4. How to manage or disable cookies

  • You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings.
  • Disabling certain cookies may impact the experience on some websites.

5. More information

For more details about personal data processing, see our Privacy Policy.
